Introduction

The International Isotope Society UK Group 32nd Annual Symposium is a one day event covering the synthesis and applications of isotopically labelled compounds to be held on Friday, 13th November 2026 at The Møller Institute in Cambridge, UK.

Highlights of the meeting will include talks from Professor Gill Reid, CBE​ (University of Southampton​, UK) and Professor Troels Skrydstrup (Aarhus University, Denmark).

Topics will include radiopharmaceuticals, catalysis, organic synthesis, tritium & carbon-14 synthesis among other topics. A full list of speakers will be available shortly.

Flash Presentations & Poster Session

There will also be an opportunity for young chemists to present their PhD or Post-doctoral work as a poster at the meeting. From the pool of poster presentations, 4 entrants will also have the opportunity to give a flash presentation of ~5 minutes on their work. We will be offering prizes of £250 each for the best flash presentation and best poster by a post-graduate — please contact [javascript protected email address] if you would like to present a poster.

Venue

We are delighted to be returning to The Møller Institute again this year. The venue is approximately one mile from Junction 13 on the M11 motorway and a short taxi ride from Cambridge railway station with its frequent express services to Liverpool Street station and Stansted Airport. The meeting will be timed to allow most UK based people to travel to and from the symposium on the same day although accommodation is available at The Møller Centre for those travelling from afar.

Registration

The closing date for registration is Friday, 7th November 2025.

We hope you will be able to attend and help make this meeting of the IIS UK Group as successful as in previous years. A buffet lunch will be provided for all delegates.

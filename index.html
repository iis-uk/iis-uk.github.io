Introduction

The International Isotope Society UK Group 31st Annual Symposium is a one day event covering the synthesis and applications of isotopically labelled compounds to be held on Friday, 14th November 2025 at The Møller Institute in Cambridge, UK.

Highlights of the meeting will include talks from Dr Jason Lewis​ (Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center​, USA) and Dr Katherine Wheelhouse (GlaxoSmithKline, UK).

Topics will include organic synthesis, carbon/hydrogen isotope exchange, tritium and carbon-14 synthesis, stable isotope chemistry, short-lived isotopes and imaging among other topics. A list of speakers is given below and presentation titles will be added as they become available:

Poster Session

Venue

We are delighted to be returning to The Møller Institute again this year. The venue is approximately one mile from Junction 13 on the M11 motorway and a short taxi ride from Cambridge railway station with its frequent express services to Liverpool Street station and Stansted Airport. The meeting will be timed to allow most UK based people to travel to and from the symposium on the same day although accommodation is available at The Møller Centre for those travelling from afar.

Registration

The closing date for registration is Friday, 6th November 2025.

We hope you will be able to attend and help make this meeting of the IIS UK Group as successful as in previous years. A buffet lunch will be provided for all delegates.
